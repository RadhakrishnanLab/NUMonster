Cloning PCR Protocol (per Ishwar Radhakrishnan)

6/20/05

 

  1. Prepare all samples on ice

 

Into each reaction tube add:

  1. Template (use 20-50 ng of plasmid)
  2. Forward Primer (use 1 mg)
  3. Reverse Primer (use 1 mg)
  4. Add optimization buffer A, C, F, or N (5x stock – add 10 mL per 50 mL reaction)
  5. 0.5 mL Polymerase (Use Vent Polymerase (NEB) with proofreading ability)
  6. Including the 5 mL for dNTPs (to be added later) calculate and add the amount of UV/UF water needed to bring reaction to 50mL
  7. Start up PCR machine
  8. Run under user Kurt “exp 001”
  9. When unit reaches 80°C, pause the run and then add dNTP mixture (Use 5 mL of 2 mM dNTP stock)
  10. When cycle is finished, the machine will keep the tubes at 4°C for infinity.  To save on the life of the machine, place samples in the -20°C freezer until ready for the next step.
