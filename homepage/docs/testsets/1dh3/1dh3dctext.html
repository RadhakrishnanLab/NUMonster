Overview
  • Paper: J.Biol.Chem. Vol.275 #45 pp 35242-35247
  • Description: CREB bZIP complexed with 8 base-pair DNA strand.
  • Residue List: Derived from CRE Binding Specificity section of Results and Discusson. Extra interactions not mentioned in the text are referred to in the [Extra Interactions] section.

Explanation

  • Due to the symmetry of the complex, Monster was not only scrutinised for accurate DNA-Protein interactions, but for symmetrical interactions too.

  • Green indicates that the interaction listed in the paper was found.

Results

  • Monster found all the listed interactions, including the specified asymmetric interactions involving Arg 301.

  • Monster found the specified TYPE of interactions involving ARG 301. Namely a water-mediated hydrogen bond to Ade -2 and a hydrogen bond to Gua 1.

  • Only one hydrogen bond between Arg 301 and Gua 1 was found, that involving the O6 of Gua 1. The paper also specified that a hydrogen bond should exist with the N7 too.

  • Monster found all listed interactions between opposite pairs, bar the asymmetric interactions.

Conclusions

  • We conclude that Monster passed overwhelmingly, both in finding the right DNA-Protein interactions, but also in finding the symmetrical interactions AND the asymmetrical interactions, bar one.
