lab news

July, 2004


NMR Facility Manager search

The search for NMR Facility manager is progressing well. We will have the first candidate Dr. Yongbo Zhang from the University of Michigan visiting us on July 27th. Watch for further announcements. 


Post-doctoral Fellow search

The search for a post-doctoral fellow is also going well. We will have the first candidate Dr. Michael Durney from the University of Utrecht, The Netherlands visiting us on August 1st-2nd. Stay tuned.


NMR Cryoprobe grant

Our NIH shared instrumentation grant application for a cryoprobe has received a favorable score.  We are cautiously optimistic that it will be funded.


900 MHz NMR

The delivery of a 900 MHz NMR instrument is expected by the end of the year. Funding for the instrument was obtained from the NIH through a tri-institutional consortium (Northwestern, UIC, and UofC). This will be a shielded 900 MHz instrument - the first of its kind in the world - and is manufactured by Bruker Instruments, Inc.


Richard Kang's Farewell

Rich is leaving for (or coming to Northwestern) graduate school. We will be having a farewell party in early August. Formal announcements will appear shortly.
