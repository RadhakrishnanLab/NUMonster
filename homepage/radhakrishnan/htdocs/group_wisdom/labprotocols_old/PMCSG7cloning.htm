Cloning with pMCSG7

from Stols et al. Protein Expression and Purification 2002

 

A)  Design primers for PCR insert

            Forward primer: 5’– TAC TTC CAA TCC AAT GCX –3’

            Reverse primer: 5’– TTA TCC ACT TCC AAT G tta tta –3’

 

B) Linearize vector with SspI

 

1.      Digest PMCSG-7 with SspI to linearize the vector

2.      In a clean and sterile microcentrifuge tube, mix 40 units of enzyme/10 μg vector.

3.      Mix gently with pipet tip

4.      Incubate at 37oC for 10 hours

5.     Heat inactivate SspI at 65oC for 20 min.

 

C) Gel purify linearized vector and PCR insert using Qiagen Gel Extraction Kit

 

D) T4 polymerase treatment of linearized vector and insert

INSERT

1 μL 100mM DTT

0.4 μL (2.5 U/μL) T4 DNA polymerase

4.6 μL H20

4.6 μL H20

 

     Incubate for 30 min @ 22oC

     Heat inactivate T4 DNA polymerase for 20 min @ 75oC

 

E) Run a gel of the T4-treated Vector and Insert to determine concentration

 

F) Annealing

 

1)     Assemble different ratios (vector:insert) of annealing reactions in 1.5 mL tubes

eg.     1:2

1:5

5:1

2)     Incubate at 22oC for 5 min

3)     Add 1 mL of 25mM EDTA to each tube

4)     Mix by stirring with the pipet tip and continue incubating at 22oC for 5 min

 

G) Transform into NovaBlue cells and select on LB + Carb
